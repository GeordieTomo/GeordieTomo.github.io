BIZEN

about BIZEN

This game has been in development for around two years now, taking inspiration from the likes of games such as 'flower' or 'rez', BIZEN tells the story of the need for environmental change. It is primarily an open world exploration type game, where almost everything reacts to the music of the game, aiming to immerse the player in a world of chromothesia.
